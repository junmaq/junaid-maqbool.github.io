About

My research interests include Signals processing, machine learning and computer vision. There I started studying signal processing and image processing and couldn’t find anything more attractive and interesting for me. After that I even rejected job offers from Pakistan Atomic Energy Commission (PAEC) the most desired job in government sector and Pakistan Oil Refinery Limited (PRL) a well known company in oil sector.
